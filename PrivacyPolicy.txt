Privacy Policy for Neomorph Calculator:

This Privacy Policy explains how Neomorph Calculator ("the App") handles user data and privacy. The App is designed to function as a simple calculator, without collecting or storing any personal information from users.

Information Collection and Use:

The Neomorph Calculator app does not collect, store, or share any personal information or user data. It operates solely as a calculator application on the user's device, without requiring access to any personal data or device features beyond the basic functionality of performing calculations.

Permissions:

The App does not require any special permissions or access to your device's features, such as camera, microphone, contacts, or location services. It operates within the standard permissions granted to calculator applications.

Data Security:

Since the App does not collect or store any user data, there is no need for data security measures. All calculations and operations are performed locally on the user's device and are not transmitted or stored elsewhere.

Third-Party Services:

The Neomorph Calculator app does not integrate with or utilize any third-party services that may collect or handle user data.

Children's Privacy:

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. It is a general-purpose calculator application suitable for all ages.
